Out of many technologies, I chose three.

AR, VR, and 3D simulation aren't the only tools that can transform learning — but they're the ones that can do it now, in real classrooms, at this moment in time.

01 · AR

AR Augmented Reality

Layers digital content over the real world, accessed through tablets or phones — no headset needed.

~ a heart, on the desk in front of you ~

02 · VR

VR Virtual Reality

A fully immersive virtual world, experienced through a headset. Students don't watch — they inhabit.

~ step inside the body ~

03 · 3D Sim

3D Simulation

Interactive 3D models on screen — pull apart, zoom in, rotate, watch processes unfold. Works on any device.

~ rotate, zoom, take it apart ~

Why
these
three?
01
Accessible. Already practical in real classrooms, on devices students use.
02
Well-established. Mature pedagogy & tooling, not still-emerging tech.
03
Feasible. Manageable scope for a Master's project, with time & resource constraints.
